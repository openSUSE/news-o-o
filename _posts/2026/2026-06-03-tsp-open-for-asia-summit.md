The [Travel Support Program (TSP)](https://en.opensuse.org/openSUSE:Travel_Support_Program), which is aided through donations to the [Geeko Foundation](https://geekos.org/), is now accepting applications for the [openSUSE.Asia Summit 2026](https://events.opensuse.org/conferences/oSAS26).

Funds are allocated by the foundation specifically for travel assistance for speakers attending the event.

Applications for the TSP are open now and will run until July 31, which will follow an announcement related the Call for Papers. 

People whose talks are accepted can submit a request at [tsp.opensuse.org](https://tsp.opensuse.org/events).

The TSP exists to ensure financial constraints don't prevent passionate contributors and community members from participating. 

The openSUSE.Asia Summit 2026 organizers of the summit encourage you to apply early.

For questions about the TSP process, visit the [wiki for more information](https://en.opensuse.org/openSUSE:Travel_Support_Program) and read the [Geeko Foundation's travel policy](https://en.opensuse.org/images/4/4c/Geeko_Foundation_Travel_Policy.pdf). 

Further details will be shared later about the event planning, so please pay attention to announcements for the summit.

We look forward to seeing you there!

For more details on openSUSE.Asia Summit 2026, visit [events.opensuse.org](https://events.opensuse.org/).
